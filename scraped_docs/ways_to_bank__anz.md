## Digital wallets
Easy, secure payments on the go.
Join the 1 in 4 Australians around the country who regularly leave home without their physical wallet. disclaimer You can pay in-store, online or in apps using a digital wallet on your phone or wearable device. disclaimer
It's quick, convenient and secure - and set up is easy. Just add your card details to a digital wallet on your phone, smartwatch or fitness tracker.
So next time you grab a coffee, go shopping or fill up your car at the servo, you can pay with your phone or wearable device. disclaimer
