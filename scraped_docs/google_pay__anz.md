EndNoIndex ## What is Google Pay?
Google Pay links to your ANZ credit or debit card so you can make payments easily and securely. Just add your card to Google Wallet™ (formerly Google Pay app) on your eligible phone, Pixel® Watch (smartwatch) or Fitbit device and pay for online disclaimer shopping, petrol and more in seconds.
## Why use Google Pay?
#### Speedy payments
No need to pull out physical cards or count cash. Make quick, contactless payments from the phone you’re carrying or smartwatch you’re wearing.
#### More convenient
It’s not just handy for payments. If you report your card as lost or stolen , your replacement card will automatically update in your Google Wallet
#### Safe and secure
Google Pay transactions have the same security as your other ANZ Card payments.
#### Accepted worldwide
You can use Google Pay in-store and online disclaimer , anywhere your card is accepted. Simply look for the contactless or Google Pay logos.
## Getting started with Google Pay
It’s easy. To get set up with Google Pay, all you need is:
- Your Android phone
- The ANZ App
- A Google account (you’ll already have one if you login to Gmail, YouTube or Google Ads)
- An eligible ANZ debit or credit card (you can also add multiple cards)
## How to add your ANZ card to Google Wallet
To start using Google Pay, just add your ANZ card to your digital wallet.
### In the ANZ App
### On your phone
Log in to the ANZ App
Select the account
Tap Manage
Tap Add to Wallet and follow the prompts. Sorted!
Note: If you have multiple cards you will need to swipe left to choose the card you want to add to your wallet
### On your smartwatch
Open the Google Wallet app on your smartwatch
Tap Get started
Set up a screen lock on your smartwatch (if you haven't already)
Open the Google Wallet app on your phone and follow the prompts to complete setup. Done!
## How to use Google Pay
### To make payments in-store
### On your phone
Unlock your phone
Hold it over the contactless terminal
When you see the tick on your phone screen, payment is complete. Easy!
### On your smartwatch
Open the Google Wallet app
Hover your smartwatch over the contactless terminal
When you hear a beep or feel a vibration from your smartwatch, your payment is complete. Sorted!
### To make payments onlinedisclaimeror in apps
### On your phone
Select Google Pay at checkout
Select the card you would like to pay with
Confirm your payment and you’re all done!
### To withdraw cash
### At an ANZ Smart ATM (visa debit card only)
Open the Google Wallet app
Choose the card you want to use
Tap your device at the ATM
Enter your PIN
Follow the prompts on the ATM screen. Done!
### At a participating merchant
Unlock your phone to access Google Pay
Select the card you would like to pay with
Hold it over the contactless terminal
When you see the tick on your phone screen, the transaction is complete. Easy!
## How to remove an ANZ card from Google Wallet
### On your phone
Open the Google Wallet app
Swipe to find the card you want to remove
Tap the card to select it
Tap the three vertical dots and scroll down and tap Remove card
Confirm your selection by tapping Remove . You’re all sorted!
### On your smartwatch
Open the Google Wallet app
Swipe to find the card you want to remove
Tap the card to select it
Tap the three dots and then select Remove payment method
Confirm your selection by tapping Remove . You’re all sorted!
