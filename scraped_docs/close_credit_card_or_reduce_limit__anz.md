# How to close or reduce the limit on your credit card
If you're the account holder, you may use this page to request the closure of your credit card account or to reduce your credit card account limit.
EndNoIndex ## What's your payout figure?
To close your credit card account, you'll need to pay the full outstanding balance including any accrued interest and fees. Known as a payout figure, this amount can change daily for a number of reasons, such as pending or recurring transactions, or accrued interest. You can request a payout figure, anytime, online using the ANZ App or Internet Banking. Or, you can give us a call.

## Your questions answered
### If I have an outstanding balance, will my credit card closure request be cancelled?
No, if you've requested to close your credit card account but your account is not paid in full, then we will only be able to cancel your credit card.
After your credit card is cancelled and you have paid the full outstanding balance including any accrued interest and fees, your credit card account will be closed.
### What happens if I haven't paid my full closing balance on the day I request to close my card?
If your account is not paid in full on the day you request closure then we will only be able to cancel your credit card. If your card is cancelled, you will:
- Continue to accrue interest and fees until the outstanding balance is paid in full
- Need to pay at least the minimum monthly payment by the due date each month until paid in full
- Be responsible for any transactions debited to the credit card account in accordance with any recurring transaction request or other standing authority or any other authorised link to the credit card account. For recurring payments, you must contact the merchant to arrange another payment option or to cancel the recurring payment altogether
- Receive written confirmation about card cancellation
- Continue to receive monthly statements until the outstanding balance is paid in full.
After your credit card is cancelled and you have paid the full outstanding balance including any accrued interest and fees, your credit card account will be closed.
### How will I know when my credit card account is actually closed?
Once your account is closed, you will receive written confirmation from us by post confirming your account has been closed, as well as your closing account statement.
### Is the account holder the only person who can close a credit card account?
In certain circumstances, a person authorised to act on behalf of the account holder may close an account holder's credit card account in the above ways.
### What is the minimum limit I can reduce my credit card to?
When selecting your preferred new credit limit, it can be equal to, but must not be less than, the minimum credit limit applicable to your credit card product.
