# Set a savings goal
Saving for something? Set a goal to help you save. Our goals feature in the ANZ App can help you stay on track to get to your goal.
EndNoIndex ## How to set up a savings goal
### To set a savings goal in the ANZ App
You’ll need an eligible account, an ANZ Progress Saver or ANZ Online Saver disclaimer disclaimer :
Log in to the ANZ App.
Tap For You .
Tap on Goals and follow the prompts.

Tip: Each eligible account, including those held by business customers, can only have one goal. Joint account holders will only be able to see the goal they set up.
Check your progress in the ANZ App at any time
Next time you tap on your account you’ll notice a progress bar when you check your account next. This will show you how you’re progressing towards your goal. How good is that!
## Extra tips to help save for your goals
### Save first
Payday? Cha-ching! It may help to transfer some money straight into your savings as soon as you’re paid.
Put it on auto-pilot with an automatic transfer so you’re saving regularly without even knowing. You can set this up in the ANZ App or Internet Banking.
### Be kind to yourself
Make it realistic, with a small stretch target, based on what is manageable for you to live and save.
And remember. we all go off track at one point or another. Don’t let it stop you.
### See and celebrate your progress
Check in on your goal regularly to see how you’re going and adjust your goal if needed.
Getting to 5% or 50% of your goal are both big deals. Celebrate each achievement!
### Create dedicated goals
Saving for different things? It can be helpful to create individual goals to stay focused. You can set one goal on each eligible ANZ savings account.
