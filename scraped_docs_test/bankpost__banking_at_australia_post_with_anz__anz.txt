Bank@Post – Banking with ANZ at Australia Post
Bank@Post for ANZ customers
Bank@Post is a secure service provided by Australia Post that enables ANZ customers to perform basic banking transactions at over 3,300 participating Post Offices across Australia.
ANZ customers with Visa Debit cards can:
Deposit cash (up to $8000 per transaction)
Withdraw money (up to $2000 per transaction subject to daily withdrawal limits)
Check account balance
Bank@Post for ANZ Access Visa Debit, ANZ Plus Visa Debit and ANZ Business Visa Debit card holders is available at participating Post Offices.

How it works
You can easily and securely deposit and withdraw cash at Bank@Post locations during regular business hours.
Bring your ANZ Access Visa Debit, ANZ Plus Visa Debit or ANZ Business Visa Debit card to a participating Post Office and input your PIN to complete the transactions above.
